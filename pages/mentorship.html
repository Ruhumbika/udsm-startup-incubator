Mentorship Program

Supporting student innovators through guidance and expertise

Overview

The UDSM Startup Incubator Mentorship Program is designed to connect student entrepreneurs with experienced mentors from academia and industry. The program aims to nurture innovative ideas and transform them into sustainable startups.

Objectives

  • Provide professional guidance to student entrepreneurs.
  • Enhance business and technical skills.
  • Support idea validation and product development.
  • Encourage innovation and entrepreneurship culture.

Mentorship Areas

  • Business Model Development
  • Technology and Product Design
  • Marketing and Customer Acquisition
  • Financial Planning and Investment Readiness

Program Benefits

Participants gain access to expert knowledge, networking opportunities, and continuous support throughout their startup journey. This increases the chances of success and long-term sustainability.

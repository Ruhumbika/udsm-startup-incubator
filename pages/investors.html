Pitch Preparation Tips

  • Keep your pitch deck concise (10-15 slides max)
  • Clearly define the problem and your solution
  • Highlight your unique value proposition
  • Include market size and growth potential
  • Showcase your team's expertise
  • Be clear about how much funding you need and how you'll use it

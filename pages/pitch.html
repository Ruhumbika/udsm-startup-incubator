Frequently Asked Questions

Our review committee will evaluate your submission within 7 days. If shortlisted, you'll be invited for a pitch session with our investment team. Successful applicants receive term sheets within 14 days of their presentation.

We offer seed funding ranging from TZS 5M to TZS 50M, depending on the stage and needs of your startup. Additional follow-on funding is available for promising ventures that meet their milestones.

While we prioritize UDSM students and alumni, we accept applications from all Tanzanian founders. However, UDSM affiliates receive additional benefits and preferential consideration.
